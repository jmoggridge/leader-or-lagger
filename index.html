Are you leading or are you lagging??

For values, e.g.

  year   lead(year) lag(year)
1 2001 2002 NA
2 2002 2003 2001
3 2003 2004 2002
4 2004 2005 2003
5 2005 NA 2004

lead(x)

Lifts values up. Missing values appear at the end of the vector.

 lag(x)

Pushes values down. Missing values appear at the beginning of the vector.
